as someone who's a first generation Filipino

with parents who immigrated in the 1990s

we were having conversations about this subject

the room silenced as my mom almost cried

she already has a lot on her plate anyway

my brother and i tried to reassure her

we were relieved to be naturalized already

but her heart ached for all the illegal Filipinos

they all came for opportunities outside of the Philippines

why would America abandon that idea for political reasons?

people are desperate for these kinds of opportunities

it really isn't fair to watch and do nothing

I have to keep moving forward despite the political discourse

I felt more grateful and lucky than ever before

being here is enough for me even though the world's burning

this doesn't just apply to asians but any immigrant in general

it's so painful but there isn't anything we can do right now

I stopped watching the news and focused on my life instead

what's the point of America being called "the land of milk and honey"

back when my parents both arrived here in excitement like anyone else

when this happens right after the new year out of all things?

we already embraced the truth but felt very bitter otherwise

just stay in the present and don't worry too much about everything else
